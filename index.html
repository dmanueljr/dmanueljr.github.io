About me

My background

For more than a decade, I have been working in the legal field -- particularly, U.S. immigration. I enjoy working with people, especially with immigrants just like myself. I am able to successfully manage client cases and maintain corporate client relationships. I serve as the unofficial "one-man welcoming committee" for new hires by training them and keeping a team-friendly atmosphere in an otherwise stressful law firm environment.

Behind all these, I have a strong passion for web technology. Everything, from connectivity and automation to every single inefficiencies of all the immigration case management software that I have used in my legal career, keeps me motivated to become the best web developer that I can be.

My future

My goal is to be able to advance my career as a successful web developer. I would like to join a group whose aim is to utilize technology in making life easier, one meaningful app at a time. I will use my professional experience as a foundation in web development, and will continue to improve my technical knowledge through online and neteork resources.
